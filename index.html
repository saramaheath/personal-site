ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Sara :)

I'm a full stack software engineer. Before making the switch over to programming I had a very rewarding and impactful career as a hospice massage therapist. Due to the toll that massage work can have on the body I needed to find a new path. I found that software engineering could offer many of the same gifts that my previous career had, like continued learning, new exciting challenges, work that impacts the world and more. I gained my current developer skills from Rithm School, an intense and focused 16 week coding bootcamp. There I gathered experience in languages like JavaScript, Python, SQL, HTML and CSS. I also had the opportunity to dive into frameworks and other technologies like React, React-Native, jQuery, Flask, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and Express. I am excited and ready to continue learning from my peers and all of those in the gracious developer community!
