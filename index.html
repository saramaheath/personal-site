ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Sara :)

I'm a full-stack software engineer currently working at RealtyAds, a SaaS company focused on helping commercial real estate owners improve their advertising reach to a specific audience of brokers and potential tenants. I joined RealtyAds as a Junior Software Engineer in 2022 and was promoted mid 2024 to Software Engineer. It's been an exciting journey, and I’ve had the opportunity to contribute to a dynamic team and work on meaningful projects.

Before joining RealtyAds, I graduated from Rithm School, an intensive 16-week coding bootcamp that provided me with the foundational skills to transition into the tech industry. Since then, I’ve had hands-on experience with a variety of programming languages, libraries, and technologies. I work with PHP, JavaScript, Python, Node.js, SQL, HTML, CSS, jQuery, React, Flask, Express, PostgreSQL and more.

I’m passionate about solving problems, building efficient software solutions, and continuously learning new technologies to stay on top of the ever-evolving tech landscape.
