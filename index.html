ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Sara :)

I'm a full-stack software engineer who loves turning ideas into applications that are both useful and intuitive. I work across the stack with tools like JavaScript, React, Next.js, Node, Express, Python, Flask, PHP, and SQL, and I enjoy the challenge of taking projects from concept to launch.

Before moving into tech, I worked in healthcare and wellness. That experience shaped the way I think about user experience — I approach software with a people-first mindset, aiming to build tools that feel approachable, thoughtful, and genuinely helpful.

I got my start at Rithm School, an immersive coding bootcamp, and since then I’ve been building and growing as a software engineer, contributing to production-level applications and learning something new every step of the way.

At the core, I’m motivated by solving problems, creating efficient solutions, and working on projects that make systems easier and smarter for the people using them.

Outside of coding, you can usually find me making art (mosaics, pen drawings, or tiny clay sculptures), practicing yoga, or heading out on a hike to recharge outdoors.
